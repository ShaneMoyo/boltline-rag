# What is Boltline?

Boltline is a cloud-based platform for **iterative hardware engineering**. It aims to coordinate **parts, people, and processes** in one place so teams can move quickly without losing control of configuration and traceability.

The product is positioned as an alternative to stitching together spreadsheets, ad hoc documents, and legacy manufacturing execution systems (MES). The goal is a **single source of truth** for how hardware is designed, built, tested, traced, and improved over time.

Boltline is offered to organizations beyond a single company: industries that build complex physical products—including aerospace, defense, climate technology, biotechnology, and advanced manufacturing—often need rigorous traceability and fast iteration. Public messaging emphasizes that the toolkit was **proven in demanding hardware environments** (including space hardware development) before being generalized for broader use.

For interview conversations, it helps to describe Boltline as bridging **engineering intent** (what you designed) with **as-built reality** (what actually shipped and how it was serviced).
